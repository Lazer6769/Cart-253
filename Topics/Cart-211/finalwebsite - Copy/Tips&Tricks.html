Using a VPN is the best way to keep yourself private from anyone trying to steal your information, a VPN will change where your IP address is, for example an ip address originally in canada can be changed to an IP address somewhere in asia, this will make anyone who is trying to get your information think that your somewhere in asia when in fact you are in canada. this will also benefit you when your trying to use "free websites" like trying to watch a show without paying, since you wont have your info stolen and if you VPN comes with ad blocker the ads on those websites are basically non-existent.

Encryption is the process of converting readable data into a secret code called ciphertext to prevent unauthorized access. This is achieved using an algorithm and a secret key, which is essential for deciphering the ciphertext back into its original, readable form, known as plaintext. It is a critical security tool used to protect sensitive information during storage and transmission

Audibly

Microphones are used to talk through, like talking on the phone, talking on a walkie-talkie, etc, but they can also be used to listen through. When we talk, our microphones are listening to us. They can use what we talk about to target ads towards us, or sell what we say for profit, or use our voices to train their AI models.

hidden microphones work the same way as regular microphones where they can listen to what we say, however they function more like hidden cameras where someone can place them somewhere you wouldn't notice and listen to anything you say waiting for you to talk about something note worthy like credit card info, bank info anything they can use against, and now you wonder can I say what i want or will there be punishment for what I say.

Alexa or anything company-related: Alexa or anything like it is used to listen to our every command, however when we “shut it off” it can continue to listen to our every word allowing amazon or who ever else to listen to our needs and wants, conveniently placing ads to distract and make us buy what they hear from us using our voice to gain a profit. Exploiting us for talking. To one another, should we stop talking as a whole so they don't force ads down our throats?!?
